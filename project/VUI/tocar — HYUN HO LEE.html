2016 - Workshop, Santiago Piedrafita

Designed for Driving Safety

 

The use of mobile phones while driving a car is a major cause of accidents. Operating Bluetooth or Voice control is make driving more safe and efficient, it helps the driver to concentrate on the road.

 

Proficiency: UX/UI Design, UX Research, Visual Design, Interaction Design, Mobile Design, Behavioral Design, VUI Design, Wireframe

PROBLEM

The driver uses voice recognition for safe driving and keep eyes forward. However, due to interface and functional error of the voice recognition systems, the driver watches or uses the mobile phone unnecessarily.

DESIGN PROCESS

PRODUCT GOALS

The driver need to provided the necessary information without touch the mobile phone and lead to concentrate on the road condition. It should be easy to operate speech recognition, such as music selection, email processing, dialing, and set destination when they are driving. The driver should feel lightly conversation with the passenger.

PRIMARY RESEARCH

 

  • The existing speech recognition patterns are need to confirms the user 's command by text or touch the phone.

 

  • If the command is uncertain or the voice recognition error continues, the system will send an error message, or display the search results to the user, and user need to feedback by read, find, select and touch in manually.

 

  1. Voice recognition using Push to talk ( PTT)
  2. Motion recognition
  3. Touch screen input mode

 

  • Existing speech recognition requires user adapt to speech recognition, and additional practice is required to obtain accurate results.

 

SECONDARY RESEARCH

 

Bluetooth device and Hands-free device actual influence while driving.

 

  • Talking on a hands-free phone was more distracting than talking to a passenger. Many functions in the mobile phone due to technological advances, that makes decrease the drivers concentration and the interface is inappropriate.

BEHAVIORAL VARIABLES

In order to analyze driver's driving information, driving style, career and driving pattern in unexpected variables. Driving behavior was analyzed by 10 drivers in different environments.

What points are inconvenient and bring additional manipulate phone?

Most driver had bluetooth function but they are not used to use because, the bluetooth is hard to connect every time, very distracting, recognition error and very frustrating to use.

Why drivers keep watching mobile phone?

People can speak faster than they type, but can listen much more slowly than they can read. The displays for delivering information that direct influence on the amount of unsuitable for audio due to its length.

What’s the use case for their app?

Most of drivers use music apps while driving. However, Uber and lyft drivers were frequently used to their app (Uber, Lyft) with google map for pick up and drop the passenger.

USER CONVERSATION PATTERN

When driver conversation with passenger is something that participate in naturally without being conscious. Most conversation don’t think of the rules of grammar. Driver don’t think of everything required to understand, interpret, and respond.

 

"Driver just speak, but driver don’t have to think about voice controls systems"

 

The voice recognition systems has fixed time limit of voice record. So, as soon as the voice ends, it starts searching immediately. So, if the driver can afford to say the desired information or function, the driver will be able to more focus on the driving.

VUI SYSTEM FLOW

Voice user interface is not only affected by the objective limitations of a voice recognition, but human factors play a decisive role: auditive and orientation capabilities, attention, clarity, diction, speed, pronounce and ambient noise. Speech is not recognized with an accuracy of 100%. Even humans are not able to do that.

Voice controlled listening is controlled by the short term memory. Listening to long utterances has the effect that users forget most of the information that was given at the beginning. But, because of this property, speech is ideal to deliver information without forcing the user to switch context.

FLEXIBILITY vs ACCURACY

When the driver has enough time to speak the desired information or function. The system will analyze by primary vocabulary and words patterns from the users. The VUI can delivery the accurate information to drivers.
